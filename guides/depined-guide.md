# DePINed Guide

## Overview
DePINed is a decentralized compute and bandwidth marketplace that allows users to seamlessly monetize their unused PC resources via a 1-click Chrome extension.

## Why Run DePINed?
- **Ease of Use:** Just a Chrome extension. No complicated Docker or Linux terminal commands required.
- **AI-Powered:** Supplies bandwidth and processing power directly into the decentralized supercloud for AI model training and data analysis.
- **Passive Income:** Leave the extension running in the background and earn $DePIN points.

## How to Get Started
1. **Visit the Dashboard:** Go to [depined.org](https://depined.org/).
2. **Install the Extension:** Click on the "Download Extension" button.
3. **Register / Login:** Use your preferred email or web3 wallet to create an account.
4. **Activate:** Make sure the extension is active. You will see your dashboard begin to accumulate points.
