## Why DataHive?
DataHive solves the massive data-hunger problem of AI companies. Instead of centralized servers scraping the web, DataHive uses a distributed network of user nodes (that's you!) to gather and process public data ethically. 

### Key Features:
- **Zero Investment**: Completely free to start.
- **Low Resource Impact**: Runs silently in the background on your browser or desktop without slowing down your computer.
- **High APY Potential**: Early adopters historically receive the largest airdrops in DePIN networks.

### Step 2: Install the Node
DataHive offers two ways to mine:
1. **Browser Extension**: Easiest method. Simply install the Chrome/Brave extension, log in, and it will automatically start sharing idle bandwidth.
2. **Desktop Node**: For power users. Download the desktop client for Windows or Linux. The desktop node is considered more stable and often yields a 1.5x multiplier on points.

### Step 3: Optimize Your Earnings

- **Referrals**: Share your referral link. DePIN projects heavily reward network expansion.
