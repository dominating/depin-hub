## Overview
GoMining democratizes Bitcoin mining by allowing users to purchase and hold NFTs backed by real, physical mining equipment. By holding these NFTs, you receive daily Bitcoin rewards directly to your wallet without the hassle of maintaining the hardware yourself.

## Setup Instructions
1. **Sign Up:** Create an account using this [referral link](https://cryptol.ink/pltC8g.
2. **Get an NFT:** Browse the marketplace and acquire a GoMining NFT. Each NFT represents a specific amount of computing power (TH/s) and energy efficiency (W/TH).
3. **Provide a BTC Address:** Link your Bitcoin wallet address in your account settings.
4. **Earn Daily:** Once you hold an NFT, daily BTC mining rewards will automatically accumulate. You can optionally pay maintenance fees in GoMining tokens (GOMINING) for a discount, maximizing your BTC yield.
