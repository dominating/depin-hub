## Overview
UpRock is a DePIN project that allows users to share their unused internet bandwidth and compute power to help train AI models and perform web scraping tasks. It is primarily focused on mobile users, offering a seamless background mining experience on smartphones.

## Setup Instructions
1. **Sign Up:** Create an account using this [referral link](https://cryptol.ink/gelNYc.
2. **Download the App:** Install the UpRock app on your Android or macOS device (iOS coming soon/via Safari workaround).
3. **Log In:** Sign in with the account you just created.
4. **Start Mining:** Leave the app running in the background while connected to Wi-Fi to start earning $UPT tokens.
