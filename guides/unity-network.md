# Unity Network (Mobile DePIN)

Unity Network is an emerging DePIN project that turns your idle Android smartphone into a telecom-focused edge verification node. Very similar to **Acurast** or **UpRock**, Unity allows you to earn passive income by dedicating your phone's compute power and network connectivity to perform real-world telecom tasks.

## What is Unity Network?

Telecom companies and online services constantly need to verify their network intelligence, routing, and uptime from consumer-grade connections. Instead of deploying expensive physical sensors, they crowdsource this data via the **Unity Edge Network**.

By running the Unity App on your Android device, you become a **ULO (Unity License Operator)**. Your phone will periodically wake up in the background to process verification tasks, generating **Unity Points** that are redeemable for crypto rewards.

## Setup Guide

1. **Download the App:** Find the "Unity App" by Unity Network on the Google Play Store (`io.unitynodes.unityapp`).
2. **Claim a License:** To start earning, you must bind your device to a ULO License (available via the Unity Rewards portal).
3. **Background Operations:** Once the license is bound, your phone will automatically fetch and execute telecom connectivity tasks in the background.
4. **Best Practices:**
   - Use an old, spare Android phone that remains plugged into power.
   - Connect it to a stable Wi-Fi network (or a cheap unlimited data plan if testing cellular routing).
   - Ensure you disable "Battery Optimization" for the Unity App in Android settings so the OS doesn't kill the background process.

## Why it's like Acurast
Like Acurast, Unity Network leverages the secure enclaves and processing power of everyday mobile phones to build a decentralized computing/verification layer, drastically reducing infrastructure costs for enterprise clients while rewarding the hardware owners.
