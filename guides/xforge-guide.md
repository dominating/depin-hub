# XForge / XProtocol Node Guide

**Project:** XForge
**Description:** The world's first node-operated blockchain DePIN smartphone.
**Airdrop Status:** Active rewards and token incentives.

## Overview
XForge, powered by XProtocol, allows users to earn passive income, airdrops, and crypto rewards directly from their smartphone acting as a node.

## How to Get Started
1. **Acquire/Setup:** Participate in the XForge device ecosystem or install the XProtocol node app on a compatible device.
2. **Run the Node:** Keep the device online and connected to generate network value.
3. **Earn Rewards:** You'll earn direct rewards and qualify for airdrops simply by providing uptime and participating in the DePIN network.
