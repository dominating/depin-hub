# Nodle Network Guide

**Project:** Nodle
**Website:** [nodle.com](https://cryptol.ink/NFpWiy
**Description:** Decentralized wireless network for IoT connectivity.
**Airdrop Status:** Ongoing token emissions for network coverage.

## Overview
Nodle utilizes the Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) capabilities of everyday smartphones to build a decentralized global IoT network. It's one of the easiest DePINs to enter with zero hardware cost (just your phone).

## Setup Instructions
1. **Download the App:** Get the Nodle app on your iOS or Android device.
2. **Grant Permissions:** Allow the app to use Bluetooth and location services (necessary for proving network coverage).
3. **Run in Background:** Leave the app running as you go about your day.
4. **Earn $NODL:** Your phone connects to nearby IoT devices and transmits small packets of data, earning you $NODL tokens in return.
