# NodeGo AI Guide

**Project:** NodeGo AI
**Website:** [nodego.ai](https://cryptol.ink/Fw3xou
**Description:** A browser extension that lets you earn tokens passively by keeping your device online and sharing unused bandwidth to power AI models.
**Airdrop Status:** Confirmed airdrop for early participants providing network bandwidth.

## Overview
NodeGo AI is an emerging DePIN project that taps into your unused internet bandwidth to help train and run decentralized AI models. By simply running their lightweight browser extension, you earn a stake in the AI revolution while your device stays online.

## Setup Instructions
1. **Visit the Website:** Head over to [nodego.ai](https://cryptol.ink/Fw3xou and sign up for an account.
2. **Download the Extension:** Install the NodeGo AI DePIN Connect extension from the Chrome Web Store.
3. **Log In:** Open the extension and log in with your newly created account credentials.
4. **Earn Passively:** Leave your browser running in the background. The extension will automatically share your unused bandwidth and accumulate points/tokens towards the upcoming airdrop.
